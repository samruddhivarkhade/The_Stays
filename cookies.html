Cookies Policy

Learn how we use cookies to enhance your experience and keep your journey smooth.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our platform. They help improve your browsing experience and remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to personalize your experience, remember login sessions, show relevant offers, and analyze user behavior on our site.

3. Types of Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality, performance cookies for analytics, and preference cookies to remember your choices and settings.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by trusted third-party services, such as analytics providers, to help us understand user trends and improve our services.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies via your browser settings at any time. Disabling some cookies may affect certain features of our platform.

6. Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. We always ensure that your data remains secure and private.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update our cookies policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here, so please review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

For questions regarding our cookies policy or how your data is used, please contact us at support@thestays.com.
